About Me

A Little About Me...

Hello! My name is Melinda Tracy. I have been taking photos for the past 5 years. I have come to love it so much! I moved to St. George, Utah after graduating high school. Once I got that red dirt in my shoes I could never get myself to leave! I love it here so much! In 2018 I married my Italian sweetheart Simone. Together we love to travel and be with family.

I received my bachelor's degree in visual art and human communication at Dixie State University. This is where I learned the fundamentals and basics of photography. I was also lucky enough to receive an internship with The Walt Disney Company taking photos in Orlando, Florida. This was an amazing experience that I LOVED and helped me to grow so much as a photographer and videographer.

My favorite pictures are the ones that are unplanned and spontaneous. I love the moments that show the real beauty in our everyday lives. If you would like any more information feel free to send me an email and I will respond as soon as possible!

I can't wait to see what moments we can capture together!
